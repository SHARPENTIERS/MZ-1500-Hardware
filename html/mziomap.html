Memory map

PCG bank is only MZ-1500.
The expansion ROM area is undefined in MZ-700 (empty area; used by ROMs on boards such as MZ-1R12, MZ-1E05, MZ-1E14, etc.), and the second monitor ROM 9Z-502M is placed in MZ-1500.
In the memory mapped I/O area, only E000h-E00Fh is actually reserved and E000h-E00Bh is used. E00Ch-E00Fh is not defined. E010h-E7FFh is undefined in MZ-700, but MZ-1500 can read FFh written in ROM.
F000h-FFFFh of PCG bank is not defined. When PCG bank is switched to CGROM, the actual contents of CGROM are in the range of D000h-DFFFh, and the range of E000h-EFFFh is the mirror image.

While planes are called blue, red and green, each plane encodes a bit among the three bits of a palette number. Palette #0 is transparency. Although E1h and E3h do not close the PCG bank, switching between the DRAM bank and VRAM / KEY / TIMER bank is performed on the back, and is reflected when the PCG bank is closed at E6h. For example, when D000h-FFFFh is in the DRAM bank state and E5h Å® E3h Å® E6h OUT, the VRAM / KEY / TIMER bank state is established.
E4h closes the PCG bank.
